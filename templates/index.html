The process

Three simple steps to
change a child's day

From your home to a hospital ward in just days. No logistics hassle, no cost to you.

01
📦

List your toy

Upload a photo, describe the toy's condition and age group. Takes under 2 minutes. We accept soft toys, puzzles, games, art kits, and more.

02
🔗

We match & verify

Our team reviews listings and matches toys to hospital requests based on age group, condition, and urgency. Safety is our top priority.

03
✨

Joy gets delivered

We arrange free pickup or drop-off. You get a photo update when your toy reaches a child. Track your toy's journey in real time.
